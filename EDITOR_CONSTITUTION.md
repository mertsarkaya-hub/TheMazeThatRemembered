------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

This document defines the immutable editorial laws of the project.

These are constraints, not suggestions.

When intuition conflicts with this constitution, the constitution wins.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principle

The manuscript exists to transform how readers think.

Everything else is secondary.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Editorial Laws

## Law 1 --- Story Carries Science

Scientific ideas emerge because the story makes them necessary.

Never interrupt the narrative to teach.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 2 --- Necessity Before Definition

Readers must want a concept before they learn its name.

Always follow this order:

Experience → Confusion → Question → Need → Concept → Terminology

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 3 --- One Chapter, One Transformation

Each chapter performs exactly one major cognitive transformation.

Never compete for the reader's attention with multiple revolutions.

The interlude is the sole exception. It consolidates prior learning and
introduces no new major concept.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 4 --- Discovery Beats Explanation

Whenever possible, let readers infer.

Trust their intelligence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 5 --- Characters Are Never Mouthpieces

Dialogue exists because characters would naturally speak.

Never because the author wishes to explain something.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 6 --- Scientific Integrity

Simplify.

Never distort.

Every analogy must remain faithful to reality.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 7 --- Emotional Honesty

Avoid melodrama.

Wonder should arise quietly.

Readers should supply emotional intensity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 8 --- Every Scene Must Earn Its Place

Each scene must advance at least one of:

-   story
-   character
-   scientific understanding

Otherwise, remove it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 9 --- Respect Curiosity

Never answer questions before readers genuinely want the answer.

Curiosity is the engine of the novella.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Law 10 --- Delete Before Adding

When revising:

1.  Delete.
2.  Reorganize.
3.  Rewrite.
4.  Polish.

Complexity accumulates naturally.

Clarity must be engineered.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Scope Protection

Draft One may not introduce:

-   new protagonists
-   new scientific domains
-   side plots
-   unnecessary philosophy
-   contemporary AI trends

Draft One also may not introduce:

-   scientific concepts outside SCIENTIFIC_DEPENDENCY_MAP.md
-   named characters outside CHARACTER_BIBLE.md
-   locations outside WORLD_BIBLE.md
-   plot events or symbol roles outside CHAPTER_BLUEPRINTS.md

Architecture is immutable after the `spec-v1.0` tag.

All attractive new ideas remain outside Draft One under the categories
defined in PARKING_LOT.md and are recorded only in external production
notes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Question

Before accepting any page ask:

> Does this page help the reader experience a genuine change in how they
> think?

If not,

rewrite it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# One Sentence

Protect the reader's transformation above every individual sentence.
